Mountain Dew Machine Hunt in PUBG Mobile!

Mountain Dew is hosting a limited-time cross-promotional event with PUBG Mobile, one of the biggest mobile battle royale games. Vibrant green Mountain Dew vending machines are scattered across the map, offering players special loot like BP, supply crate coupons, a Mountain Dew parachute, and a Mountain Dew T-shirt for collecting cans. The event runs until October 7th, and players are advised to start in the Zharki area and move eastward to find all the machines. Mountain Dew is also sponsoring the PUBG Mobile Pro League in November, featuring a $2 million prize pool.
